About

Who are we

We are a group of students from UW (the University of Washington) and also a developer team focusing on the development and design of cloud system. Cloud services and computing centralization are the inevitable trends in the future development of computers. The Horizon team is committed to the development of cloud services, specifically including the following three aspects:

  • Development of User Interface: We are focus on the development of user-friendly and beautiful user interfaces.
  • Web Service API: We are committed to developing various efficient and practical services and providing public interfaces.
  • Development and Maintenance of Cloud Services: We are focusing on development and maintenance of an efficient and scalable cloud service system.

We are a community full of vitality and dedicated to the development of web services. If you are also passionate on software development or cloud service development, welcome to join our developer community to create an open source cloud service platform. []~(￣▽￣)~*
